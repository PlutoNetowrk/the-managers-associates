Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are some common queries from our clients.

Q1. What services does The Managers and Associates provide?

We provide end-to-end services in Taxation, GST, Income Tax Return (ITR) filing, Audits, Accounting, Company Incorporation, Business Advisory, Project Reports, and Legal Advisory (Corporate Law, Compliance & Contracts).

Q2. Who can take your services?

Our services are designed for individuals, startups, small businesses, NGOs, and large enterprises. Whether you need help with personal tax filing or complete business compliance, we cater to all.

Q3. Why should I hire a CA firm instead of a local accountant?

A CA firm provides professional expertise, legal compliance assurance, and strategic financial insights that go beyond bookkeeping. We ensure accuracy, reduce risk, and help businesses grow with confidence.

Q4. Do you provide legal services along with accounting and tax?

Yes. As a unique firm led by a Chartered Accountant and Advocate, we provide integrated CA + Legal solutions so clients don’t have to run between different advisors.

Q5. How do you ensure confidentiality of client information?

Confidentiality and ethics are the foundation of our practice. All client data is handled with strict security and professional responsibility.

Q6. Can you help in company registration and compliance?

Absolutely. We handle Company Incorporation, ROC filings, Secretarial Compliance, and Business Setup Advisory for startups and businesses.
